Detailed Description ¶

The PySide.QtGui.QGraphicsSceneResizeEvent class provides events for widget resizing in the graphics view framework.

A PySide.QtGui.QGraphicsWidget sends itself a PySide.QtGui.QGraphicsSceneResizeEvent immediately when its geometry changes.

It’s similar to PySide.QtGui.QResizeEvent , but its sizes, PySide.QtGui.QGraphicsSceneResizeEvent.oldSize() and PySide.QtGui.QGraphicsSceneResizeEvent.newSize() , use PySide.QtCore.QSizeF instead of PySide.QtCore.QSize .

QCommonStyle¶

Inherited by: QWindowsStyle, QPlastiqueStyle, QCleanlooksStyle, QGtkStyle, QMotifStyle, QCDEStyle

Detailed Description¶

The PySide.QtGui.QCommonStyle class encapsulates the common Look and Feel of a GUI.

This abstract class implements some of the widget’s look and feel that is common to all GUI styles provided and shipped as part of Qt.

Since PySide.QtGui.QCommonStyle inherits PySide.QtGui.QStyle , all of its functions are fully documented in the PySide.QtGui.QStyle documentation.
